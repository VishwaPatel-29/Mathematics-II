## UNIT 1: Differential & Multivariable Calculus

### 1. Find d/dx (x³ + 5x² − 2x + 1)

**Solution:**

d/dx (x³) = 3x²

d/dx (5x²) = 10x

d/dx (−2x) = −2

d/dx (1) = 0

**Answer:**

3x² + 10x − 2

---

### 3. Find ∂z/∂x

### 5. Find Gradient
